  • About me

    I am a full stack software engineer with a strong Javascript background. My experiences include technologies such as Node.js, Express, Socket.IO, AngularJS, MongoDB, Redis, LevelDB, Jade, EJS, D3.js, Gulp, Grunt, Drupal, Convio / Blackbaud, as well as developing Chrome Extensions, RESTful API endpoints and scalable backends. I am also experienced in Linux server administration, DNS, Load balancer configuration as well as executing load and stress testing of server infrastructures and application performance. I have a passion for thinking outside the box and love to tackle challenges together with other talented and enthusiastic engineers. Nothing is more inspiring and satisfying than seeing ideas become reality.
